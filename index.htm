The FP&A Agent

Are you stuck in spreadsheets while your leaders wait days for answers? The FP&A Agent automates FP&A so you move from data to decisions in minutes.

Upload your financial documents. Our AI agent reviews your actuals, budgets, and forecasts, runs the heavy analysis, and returns a complete readout: trends, risks, and clear next steps. Raw numbers become guidance you can act on—fast.

Launch the FP&A Agent Access is restricted (invite-only). You will need an Access GUID to complete registration.
