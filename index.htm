The FP&A Agent

The FP&A Agent from Insight Minds AI is an intelligent platform designed to revolutionize financial planning and analysis for modern enterprises.

Instead of spending hours manually analyzing spreadsheets and financial statements, you upload your financial documents and the agent provides comprehensive analysis, insights, and recommendations — turning raw financial data into clear, strategic guidance for faster, smarter decisions.

How it works

Three simple steps

01

Upload

Upload your financial documents (Excel, PDFs, or financial statements) containing actuals, budgets, and forecasts.

02

Analyze

The agent processes your documents and performs comprehensive financial analysis across multiple dimensions.

03

Explore

Use an intuitive dashboard to drill down, compare scenarios, and review AI-generated insights and recommendations.

What it does

Capabilities

Capability Description
Automated Financial Analysis Automatically calculates 40+ key financial ratios, identifies trends, and flags anomalies across your financial statements.
Multi-Dimensional Insights Analyzes by legal entity (for consolidation) and by management structure (for operational accountability), with drill-down from company level to cost centers.
Scenario & Strategic Planning Compare Best Case, Base Case, and Worst Case scenarios to understand the impact of strategic decisions.
Management-Ready Reporting Generates clear reports explaining key performance drivers and providing AI-driven recommendations for decision-makers.
Key features

Built for finance teams and decision-makers

Feature Description
Dual Hierarchy Analysis Analyze by both legal/consolidation structure and internal management structure for a complete financial picture.
Comprehensive Ratio Library Leverage 40+ pre-built ratios across Solvency, Profitability, Liquidity, and Efficiency.
Scenario Modeling Compare Best, Base, and Worst cases side-by-side to inform strategic planning.
Period-over-Period Comparison Instantly compare periods (Actual vs. Budget, Current Year vs. Last Year) with automated variance analysis.
Interactive Dashboards Explore data through intuitive dashboards with drill-down capabilities from company level to cost center.
AI-Powered Recommendations Beyond charts and tables—clear written recommendations to guide decisions.
